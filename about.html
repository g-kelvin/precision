More About Nairobi Precision Community College

The Nairobi Precision Institute was started in 2011. By Francis Kimani and Esther Nyawira as a small bureau offering Cyber cafe services such as internet services, printing, typesetting, photocopying among others. initially the business was called Frakies Cyber until the year 2013 when several other services were introduced due to emerging consumer demands.
We have also delved into supply of internet, as Internet Service Providers (ISP) for home users, Small businesses and corporate clients. At Nairobi Precision institute we have also gone an extra mile farther to provide the best to our students and clients

Our mission

To proactively harness, train, promote and propagate a culture of positive and vibrant leadership in our society today.

Our vision

To be a role model institution that inspires hope and inculcates virtues to all.
